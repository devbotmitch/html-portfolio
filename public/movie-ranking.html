Best Movies of 2023 According to Michelle

My top 3 movies of 2023 so far.

The Little Mermaid

Disney couldn't have found a better actress to play Ariel than Halle Bailey. Her voice is magical and the movie was beautifully done.

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

The animation, the music, the action... perfection in a movie.

Blue Bettle

First time I've ever experienced such Mexican representation in a mainstream movie. It was awesome.
